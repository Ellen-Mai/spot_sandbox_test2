About the Space Planning Optimization Tool

SPOT enables automation and flexibility with the current processes. Tasks like extracting Finelines from the CPR doc, mapping finelines to POGs, and the very process of scheduling space planners is now automated. Administrators of the tool will be able to manage SPS' holidays, change category ownership of POGs, and modify deal information.
